### Key Knowledge Shifts from Chapter 20

- **Alexandria** has completed the first stage of her identity transition. She knows she is no longer a Silencer on a mission. She knows her handler is an ally, not an overseer. She knows the network accepts her. She is waiting for the word that will replace "Silencer."
- **Vara** is now fully compromised and has explicitly chosen Alexandria over the Order. Her confession marks the point of no return.
- **Doren and Tomas** are now active allies who know Alexandria's secret (her lack of reporting) and have accepted her. The network's acceptance of Alexandria is now explicit.
- **Hark and Renne** remain unaware of Alexandria's discoveries and emotional state. The dramatic irony persists: they are preparing to protect her, not knowing she is already protecting them.
- **The Order** remains in the dark, but the gap between their intelligence and reality is now a chasm. The stage is set for Hark's integration into the network (Chapter 21) and the approaching purge.
