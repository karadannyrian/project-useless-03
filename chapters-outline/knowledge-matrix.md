### Key Knowledge Shifts from Chapter 21

- **Hark** is now an active member of the network, with defined roles and relationships. He has moved from isolated mercenary to integrated asset. He has also gained an external ally in Vallus and has set evacuation plans in motion. He knows Marta is aware of his connection to Alexandria.
- **Marta** now knows the truth about Hark and Renne's past, and about Alexandria's existence (though she may not have confirmed the operative is that child). Her acceptance of Hark is conditional but significant.
- **Vallus** has been brought into the periphery of the coming conflict. He now has a specific role to play in Alexandria's evacuation.
- **The network** as a whole now has Hark's counter-surveillance expertise and is actively preparing for a potential Order sweep.
- **Alexandria** remains unaware of Hark's integration or his true relation to her. The dramatic irony persists: she is protecting the network, and the network (including her father) is preparing to protect her, but none of them know she has already crossed over.
- **Renne** is about to have her chapter (22) where she and Hark begin to rebuild their alliance. She still doesn't know Alexandria is a Silencer, or that Alexandria has discovered her identity.
