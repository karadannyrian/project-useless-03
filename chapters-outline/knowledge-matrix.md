### Key Knowledge Shifts from Chapter 25

- **Alexandria** has fully committed to protecting Hark and Renne. She has witnessed the true scope of Hark's power and the cost he is willing to pay. Her internal shift is complete; she is no longer the Order's weapon. She does not yet know Hark is her father, but she is closer to understanding what he means to her.
- **Vallus** has seen Hark's magic and chosen to protect the secret. He is now a trusted ally in a way he wasn't before—he knows the stakes are real, even if he doesn't understand the full picture.
- **Kel** has heard Alexandria and Vallus on the ridgeline, adding another layer to his understanding of the situation. His distrust of Hark has eroded entirely.
- **The network** now knows the advance team is eliminated and Hark is returning alive. They have gained critical time.
- **The dramatic irony** is at its peak: Alexandria is protecting her father without knowing he is her father; Hark is protecting his daughter without knowing she has witnessed his fight; Renne is preparing to heal Hark without knowing her daughter has chosen them all. The next rupture will be Vara's reception of the kill order (Chapter 26), which will escalate the story into its final act.
